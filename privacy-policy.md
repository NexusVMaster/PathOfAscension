# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Just A Rock (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected and used.

## Information We Collect
The App does not require users to provide personal information such as name, email address, or location.

Limited information may be collected automatically, including device type, operating system, and anonymous usage data.

## Advertising
The App displays advertisements provided by third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob.
These services may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data to display advertisements.

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device’s privacy settings.

## Analytics
The App may collect anonymous analytics data to understand how the App is used and improve performance.

## Data Storage
All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not store personal data on external servers.

## Children’s Privacy
The App is not directed to children under the age of 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Data Sharing
We do not sell personal information. Limited data may be shared with advertising and analytics providers solely to operate the App.

## Changes
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
